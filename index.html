Privacy Policy – Galaxy Rush

Effective Date: 27/01/2026

Galaxy Rush respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information is collected and how it is used.

1. Information We Collect

Galaxy Rush does not collect personal information such as your name, email address, phone number, or precise location.

However, the game stores the following information:

  • Player score data
  • A unique device or player identifier (if applicable)
  • Basic technical information necessary to process score submissions (such as IP address, automatically provided during API communication)

This information is collected solely for gameplay functionality, including saving and displaying high scores.

2. How We Use Information

The collected data is used only to:

  • Store and display player scores
  • Maintain leaderboards (if applicable)
  • Ensure proper game functionality

The data is not used for analytics, advertising, profiling, or marketing purposes.

3. Third-Party Services

Galaxy Rush uses an external API service to store and retrieve player scores. This service processes score-related data only for enabling game functionality and does not use the data for independent marketing or advertising.

Galaxy Rush does not integrate:

  • Advertising SDKs
  • Analytics tools
  • Social media SDKs
  • Tracking technologies

4. Children’s Privacy

Galaxy Rush does not knowingly collect personal information from children or adults. The game only stores gameplay-related score data necessary for functionality.

If you believe that personal information has been inadvertently collected, please contact us so it can be removed.

5. Data Retention

Score data is retained only as long as necessary to support gameplay features such as leaderboards. Data may be deleted periodically or upon request.

6. Data Security

Reasonable technical measures are used to protect stored score data from unauthorized access or misuse.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to request deletion of your stored score data. You may contact us to make such a request.
